# UC-013: Client Audio Playback

- **UC ID:** UC-013
- **Title:** Client Audio Playback
- **Priority:** P1
- **Estimated Complexity:** M

## Description

As a client user, I want to hear the host's audio played back on my speakers or
headphones with minimal latency so that game audio is synchronized with the video stream.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Audio received from the host is decoded and played through the client's audio output
2. Audio playback latency (network arrival to speaker output) is under 5 ms
3. Audio and video are perceptually synchronized (no noticeable lip-sync drift)
4. Playback handles brief network interruptions gracefully (short silence, not noise)
5. Client audio volume is controllable independently from the host

## Technical Approach

Decode the audio stream received from UC-012 and play it through the client's audio
subsystem. See ADR-002 for the audio pipeline architecture.

## Dependencies

- UC-012 (Host Audio Streaming)
- **ADR-002** (low-latency audio architecture)
